Smart City Solutions

Smart cities are comprised of a “system of systems.” These can include smart lighting systems, building automation systems, emergency management systems, security and access control systems, intelligent grids, renewable power, water treatment and supply, transportation, and more.

In the Industrial Internet of Things architecture, smart city platforms reside at the enterprise level. These platforms perform many functions, including analytics, remote asset monitoring, performance management, decision support and/or presentation components. At a minimum, the functionality of a smart city platform must include:

• Visualization

• Application Enablement

• Data management

Parking Management Guidance System

Solution / System Description

Uzaro parking management and guidance solution provides management of vehicle traffic and access control to parking areas.

Boom gate barriers are located at the entrance and exit of parking areas to avoid unauthorized access. The vehicle mode of access can be by driver’s face recognition, QR code or RFID card and sticker. LED displays are also provided which displays parking slot vacancy and direction.

User Benefit

• Manage and control vehicle traffic inside the parking areas specially on peak hours

• Provide direction and information on available or vacant parking slots

• Integration to Property Management system for Real time monitoring of Parking slots and POS integration.
